Why I love stories so much

Stories tap into a fundamental human desire to explore and experience new things.

They transport us to fantastical worlds or immerse us in relatable situations, all from the comfort of our couch.


The thrill of suspense, the satisfaction of a character's growth, and the emotional connection we forge with them keep us engaged.
Stories are like mini-adventures, offering a fun escape from reality while leaving us with a deeper understanding of ourselves and the world around us.

Why me?

Yaniv Rosenfeld-Cohen

Why should you choose me out of hundreds of other candidates? Because I can do this:

I compiled and delevered a 45 minute presentation on my favorite genre books for a national event to a live audience. It was extremely challenging with many creteria and moving parts but also gratifying.

In my eyes, it was a lot like a standard SDLC:

  1. Acting as a one man show, I had the privelage of coming up with the initial idea for the product.
  2. Then I analyzed the probability and demand for it.
  3. Next, I created the basic story flow both visually and verbally.
  4. After that, I run a demo, and received feedback from peers on the content and the technical aspects in order to deliver the best product to the client.
  5. Lastly, I implemented the feedback and got great responses from the audience.
  6. While there's always room for growth and improvement, I do see this event and process as a great success in front of the client!
